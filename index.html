Universal City Noise Monitoring Manager

This involved overseeing a team of technicians who collected and analyzed sound level data across the property. I was directly involved in calibrating our monitoring equipment, scheduling personnel for data collection, and conducting detailed analyses of sound levels to determine their source and ensure they did not exceed permissible limits. My role required a proactive approach to identify potential noise issues, troubleshoot equipment malfunctions, and manage personnel to ensure efficient and accurate data collection. I also played a key role in communicating findings to stakeholders and ensuring compliance with regulations. .
